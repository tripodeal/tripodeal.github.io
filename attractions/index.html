Half the fun of travel is the aesthetic of lostness

Lured to the option of escaping the reality of the world we humans attempt to explore the unknown fantasies very often and sometimes we end up in some of the stunning places existing on planet earth we never knew about. Dandeli, is one of the divine creations of God that is still hidden and preserved. Let's make a deep dive of what to explore.
BAPELI BACKWATERS
Tranquility at its apex, bapeli backwaters situated on the river kali is an abode of dolphins and nature's elegance. Just the kind of stop you want to spend with your near and dear... Take a breathe and listen to the symphony of mother nature surrounded by a spectacular view on all sides. You are surged to be awed.
MOULANGI ECO PARK
Nature at its surreal best, water rides to unleash the kid in you, complemented by heavenly landscapes, The Eco park is a perfect antidote for all age groups, from the unique flying of drones to campfires under the starlit sky... what more aberrance do you want in life?
SYNTHERI ROCKS
On Kissing the kanneri, the rocks whisper the melody of a soothing tune. That's Syntheri in one line. A unique, one of a kind place, situated in the midpoint of Dandeli's wildlife sanctuary, Syntheri rocks forms one of the aesthetically pleasing venues keeping your photography prowess on toes all day long.
SUPA DAM
Known to be the second-largest dam in Asia, Supa dam is an epitome of surreal beauty blended with the perfect structure of water flow and landscape beyond just yearns the tourist to visit it once again. You just can't miss one of the incredible pieces of nature's marvel:- The jaw-dropping sunset which just hooks your eyeball attention for minutes and hours. Just insanely beautiful!
CROCODILE PARK
Known as the abode of spine chilling reptiles in its natural habitat, the crocodile park is a home of lush green picturesque locale. So have the courage, jump the gun of your camera to capture the bliss behind the storm of the reptiles.
DISNEY PARK
Oh, what a glitzy-blitzy world of Superheroes, From digital streaming platforms to theatrical plays, we all, wanted to see that moment when our favorite superheroes buries the enemy into dust the kid in us claps. What if you are given a chance to meet them physically... Disney park in Dandeli has all the action superheroes a kid craves about apart from everyone's favorite Chota bheem... You child would etch this place in his travel memory, be rest assured!
