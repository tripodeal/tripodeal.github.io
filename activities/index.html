There is a lot of adventures out there, waiting for us to live them!

We at TRIP-O-DEAL immensely strive to give a perfect blend of unique nail-biting thriller rides and calm soothing adventure sports that give every tourist a wholesome experience to travel one of the camouflaged beautiful landscapes of our country.
WHITE WATER RAFTING
The adrenaline rush, High Octane drama, that kick you feel in your nerves... Against the erratic currents, white water rafting in Dandeli a 12km long stretch in the ever-gorgeous kali river is nothing short of an edge of seat thriller on waterbed where you are expected to be at your pinnacle in presence of mind but trust us you are in for a spine chilling, nerve-wrecking experience that would the challenge the fear in you. After all these are the memories you want to take back home and cherish for an eternity.
KAYAKING
River kali is omnipresent and is a hub of adventure sports and highflying moments in your trip to Dandeli. For those who are not aware of kayaking it's mostly a solo/duel paddling and rowing of a boat that indulges you in water adventure. A unique enthralling experience to row your kayak in the pristine waters of Kali.
RAPPELING
Situated in the proximity of Supa dam, rappelling is one of the rarest and an extreme adventure sport that usually in the Bahubalian territory of fantasy where with the help of a rope you climb skiddy wet rocks and mountains in the backdrop of a serene waterfall. An incredible yet mesmerizing adventure sport that takes your breath away. Not an experienced climber but still want to try, don't worry we have covered it for you with the help of an instructor so you never miss to treasure a never before experience.
RIVER BOATING
Clear waters, insanely beautiful backdrop just a soothing boat ride is all you need. In the river kali at Dandeli gives you a perfect family ride that encompasses all the incredible ambiance and the luxury of Tranquility at your feet.
NATURAL JACUZZI
Imagine and feel that sweet-smelling aroma of morning waters that pass through you, just kissing you back and forth in a cycle. That is what natural jacuzzi is, Dandeli provides you fast-moving rapids of river kali that gives you a perfect bath and a perfect candid snapshot clicked!
JUNGLE WALK
The pleasant-smelling redolence of morning stroll pass the woods, the tiny droplets of dew crystals on the lush green leaves, and the evergreen charm of a tiny jungle, the earth never ceases to mesmerize us with its divine gifts of nature and Dandeli is one of the exemplary pearls of that gift. We at Dandeli provide jungle walks in the exotic ambiance in our resorts that can make up for a lazy day.
NIGHT CAMPFIRES
The magnificence of a well lit night sky is just impeccable at Dandeli. So, if you are still not tired of a hectic day out or are lethargic enough to move out, the in-resort campfires gives you a stellar moonlight dinner and Stargazing experience with your loved ones in a tiny camp, the one you dreamt and fantasize about during your college days is finally coming true.
CYCLING
A lost recreation with the hustle and bustle of cities Dandeli never ceases to Amaze the options you can explore. A mountain biking activity that has its own thrills and frills with all the fun in one of the roughest terrains for a 7-10km ride you are in for one of the toughest cycling rides you have ever taken part of.
RIVER CROSSING
Dream of high flying exhilarating Journey through the lush green trees with a shadow of mountains in the background and the melody of the river passing beneath you. You have come to the right place, Zipline in Dandeli is one of the safest and a mind-bending experience that is sure to take a prominent place in your moments' gallery back home.
ZORBING
Another exemplary adventure sport that is rarest of its kind in our camouflaged tourist vacay destination is zorbing. Water zorbing is an insanely unique walk inside an orb revolving yourself 360° for a while and with the capricious currents you are in for a roller-coaster ride on water. Another must-visit on your Dandeli vacay checklist.
RIVER SWIMMING
Swimming is a luxury most of the people on a travel visit comply with and we at Dandeli provide one of the most cleanest and safest waters that gives you perfect experience for your water recreational activity.
